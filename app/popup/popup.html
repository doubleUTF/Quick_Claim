Directions

Standard mode:
Go to the Profiles tab to setup and save data for commonly used CPT codes. Ensure you are on a supported site(OA, MD-Online) and at the HCFA-1500 form section. Go to Claim Form tab, enter CPT codes and dates. Click Fill Form button and verify data is correct. Quick Claim will automatically provide a date of service for all CPT codes. If this is undesired, enter data manually and use Manual mode.

Manual Mode:

Go to Claim Form tab and check Manual Mode(Not yet implemented). You will enter data manually, but with the added benefit of having the option of automatically skipping fields you know you don't use.
