Directions

If your data has many variations, use the 'Omit Fields' mode in settings and enter data manually. Otherwise, go to the settings tab and enter data for commonly used CPT codes and click save when finished. Ensure you are on a supported site(OA, MD-Online). Go to Claim Form tab and enter CPT codes.

About

Entering electronic medical claims on sites such as Office Ally or MD-Online requires tedious and repetetive data entry. Quick Claim is a Chrome extension meant to save time by remembering your settings and automatically filling out the dense portion of the form (Field 24 of CMS 1500). Quick Claim is best when entering same, repeating data. In most cases, the only data that changes are dates of service and diagnoses.

Shortcut key: Ctrl+Q
