General

Fill out forms on native site manually and allow disabling of specific columns to allow automatic tabbing between input fields.

Select all diagnosis codes for every CPT code.

Directions

  1. Ensure you are on a supported site(OA, MD-Online) and at the HCFA-1500 form section. The status bar on the Claim Form tab will indicate this.
  2. Fill out sections 1-21 either by loading saved patient data or entering new data.
  3. Go to the Profiles tab to setup and save data for commonly used CPT codes. These codes are not specific to any patient or site and can thus be used for any patient.
  4. Go to Claim Form tab, enter CPT codes and dates. Click Fill Form button and verify data is correct. Quick Claim will automatically provide each date of service for all CPT codes. If this is undesired, enter data manually.
